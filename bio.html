Bio

Hugo Peyron is a product builder, developer, and designer. He joined Voodoo in 2016, where he co-founded the publishing department during the hyper-casual era. Over several years, he worked with more than 200 mobile game studios worldwide, and the games he signed generated over 400 million downloads.

He later moved into product management and studio building. He is the founder of Cortex, a Voodoo-backed studio, where he focuses on building high-scale mobile products without lowering the bar on taste or depth. Rather than shipping isolated features, he builds systems designed to compound over time.

He is currently building The Almanac, a daily puzzle game exploring how depth, taste, and long-term thinking can survive at mobile scale.

He writes to sharpen his thinking and to understand, over time, what actually makes products last.
